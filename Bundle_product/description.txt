	Product bundle App

==> language(English)

==> content detail:
	
	Create and sell custom product bundles with fixed pricing directly on your Shopify store.	

==> App Details:

	This Shopify app allows store admins to create custom product bundles with a fixed price.
 Admins can select multiple products, set a unique bundle price, and display it on the storefront.
Customers can view and purchase the bundle directly from the product page. 
When the "Buy Bundle" button is clicked, all items are added to the cart with the 
custom bundle price applied, ensuring a seamless and engaging shopping experience.
